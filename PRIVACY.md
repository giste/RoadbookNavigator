# Privacy Policy for Roadbook Navigator

**Last updated: July 22, 2026**

Roadbook Navigator ("the App") is designed with a focus on privacy and offline functionality. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Information We Access

### Location Data
To provide its core functionality, the App requires access to your device's location (Fine and Coarse Location). 
*   **How we use it:** We use this data solely to display your position on the map, provide navigation guidance, and calculate distances (odometer).
*   **Privacy First:** This data is processed **locally and in real-time** on your device. We **never** store your location history, nor do we transmit it to any external servers or third parties.

## 2. Information We Store

### Local Storage
The App stores the following information **locally on your device**:
*   **App Settings:** Preferences such as units of measurement or theme settings.
*   **Map Data:** Map files you choose to download for offline use.
*   **Roadbooks:** Roadbook files you import into the App.

This data is stored within the App's private storage and is not accessible by other applications. We do not have access to this data.

## 3. Data Collection and Sharing

*   **No Personal Information:** The App does not require a user account, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
*   **No Third-Party Analytics:** We do not use third-party tracking or analytics services (like Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics).
*   **No Third-Party Sharing:** Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or trade any user information with third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party libraries for core functionality:
*   **Mapsforge VTM:** Used for rendering offline maps. 
*   **OpenStreetMap (OSM):** If you choose to download map tiles, the App will connect to OpenStreetMap servers to download the files. This connection is subject to OpenStreetMap's privacy policy.

## 5. Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy and the local processing of your location data as described.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/giste/RoadbookNavigator
